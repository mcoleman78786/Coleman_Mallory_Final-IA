Welcome to The TRAA

There’s a bunch of ways to get in touch with the TRAA. If you’re with a like-minded organization or group looking for more information on the TRAA and its activities or an individual who would like to become involved with the activities by the TRAA we would like to hear from you.

Membership

You can become a TRAA member for a whole year for only just $25.00. Your membership adds another voice to our efforts in protecting and enhancing aquatic habitats in the London area and community. You also get to be apart of something Important! You’ve had a lot of enjoyment from this unique fishery. It is time for you to give back and be apart of the solution.

Fundraising

Donations are needed to continue many of the TRAA’s vital projects such as; Improvements to the TRAA Trout Hatchery, Salmonid Mentoring Program, Salmonid Monitoring Program, and Komoka Creek Hydrological Study. Please contact us if you’d like to take an active role in raising the funds needed for these and other important TRAA activities.

Members of the trout hatchery rotation know the woes we’ve had with lighting in the hatchery. Even with larger fixtures installed a few years ago, it was still frustrating on days when the mercury dipped below -15C and the fluorescent technology just teased us with its dull flicker. Enter Mike Jeffries of Guillevin Greentech in London with nice set of bright and dependable (and new!) LED fixtures. Once they were installed it was like working in well-appointed laboratory.
